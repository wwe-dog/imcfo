# Product Doctrine

## 1. Doctrine

IMCFO turns personal life into a personal company operating system.

The product promise is not “record faster” by itself. It is:

```text
record a life event
-> understand its financial meaning
-> see your operating state
-> decide what to do next
```

## 2. IMCFO Is

- A personal CFO system for ordinary natural persons.
- A company-style financial language layer for personal life.
- An AI-first input and feedback system with human confirmation.
- A mobile-first personal operating view for balance sheet, income statement, cash flow, and operating feedback.

## 3. IMCFO Is Not

- A normal bookkeeping app with a dark UI.
- A generic budget tracker.
- A tax filing product.
- An enterprise accounting SaaS.
- A backend ledger platform.
- An AI auto-posting agent.

## 4. Product Difference

Most bookkeeping products compete on speed, categories, charting, import, sync, and automation.

IMCFO must compete on interpretation:

- What did this life event do to my financial state?
- Is my personal company healthier than last month?
- Where is my cash flow pressure?
- What action should I consider next?

## 5. Current MVP Direction

Current doctrine:

- Balance Sheet, Income Statement, and Cash Flow Statement form the product skeleton.
- Simple mode translates finance into everyday Chinese.
- Professional mode preserves deeper report structure.
- AI-first record input reduces friction but does not replace financial rules.
- Current UI may use Dark Liquid CFO Style, but the style is not permanent.

## 6. Simple Mode and Professional Mode

IMCFO supports two presentation modes. Both modes run on the same financial core.

Simple mode intent:

- Help users who are not accounting professionals understand their financial state in everyday language.
- The goal is accurate understanding, not maximum simplification.
- Clarity must not come at the cost of financial truth.

Professional mode intent:

- Preserve full accounting terminology and report structure.
- Serve users who want to audit, verify, or deepen their understanding.

Fallback rule:

- When a simple mode expression would be ambiguous or misleading, the system must fall back to professional terminology.
- Users must always be able to switch to professional mode to see the underlying financial language.
- The mode switch entry point must remain accessible from any screen that displays financial conclusions.

## 7. Product Guardrails

- Do not let AI-first become “AI directly writes books.”
- Do not let visual identity become the product itself.
- Do not let report names become empty decoration.
- Do not add enterprise scope before the personal CFO loop is solid.
- Do not add login, payment, tax, or cloud ledger unless a future task explicitly authorizes and an ADR records the decision.
