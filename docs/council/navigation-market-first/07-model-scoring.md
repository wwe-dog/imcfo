## A. CFO Cockpit

High Personal CFO fit because it begins from operating state. User clarity is medium because "驾驶舱" may feel business-heavy. Good feasibility if kept to a small number of actionable signals.

## B. Operating System

Strong long-term story but weak current feasibility and simplicity. The label can invite scope creep into tasks, workflows, integrations and enterprise patterns.

## C. AI-first Input

Best AI-first score, but weak financial integrity if it over-centers unconfirmed AI output. It should be input layer, not the whole app.

## D. Financial Statement

Best financial integrity but weak consumer clarity and retention. It should be a trust/report layer.

## E. Life Event Translation

Strong differentiation and AI-first fit. Needs strict boundaries between estimate, posted fact and report result.

## F. Coach / Advisor

Good user clarity and retention potential. Risk is advice overreach and generic recommendation fatigue.

## G. Operating CFO Loop

Best overall score because it combines:

- 经营 as decision-oriented operating state.
- 报告 as authoritative financial output.
- 账本 as traceable source of truth.
- 我的 as data/privacy/settings ownership.
- 说一笔 as global AI-first action.

It avoids making AI a silo, avoids making reports the whole product, and avoids ordinary bookkeeping identity.
